For every bar you buy, we feed a child in need.

It's that simple.

We feed children in local communities and around the world.

What we give

  • What: Micronutrient Packs

    Where: Developing Countries

    Ages: 5-59 months old

    Why: Helps prevent and cure malnutrition due to vitamin, mineral and micronutrient deficiencies

  • What: Plumpy'nut Packs

    Where: Developing Countries

    Ages: 5-59 months old

    Why: Peanut-based paste with both macronutrients and micronutrients, designed to help cure acute severe malnutrition

  • What: KUTOA Bars & Kids Squares

    Where: Throughout the USA

    Ages: 5-13 years old

    Why: Provide local in-need youth with healthy, portable nutrition

  • What: Healthy Sandwiches

    Where: USA and Globally

    Ages: 5-13 years old

    Why: Provide in-need youth with fresh and nutritious meals, purchased and made locally

Why Malnutrition?

  • Malnutrition is by far the #1 cause of childhood mortalityworldwide.

  • On average, a child dies every 6 seconds. That's 5 million children a year.

  • Since 2011, KUTOA has sent almost 2000,000 meals.
